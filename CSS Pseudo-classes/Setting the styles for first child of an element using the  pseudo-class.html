Recipe of Life

  1. 4 cups of Love
  2. 2 cups of Loyalty
  3. 3 cups of Forgiveness
  4. 1 cup of Friendship
  5. 5 spoons of Hope
  6. 2 spoons of Tenderness
  7. 4 quarts of Faith
  8. 1 barrel of Laughter

Steps in the making

  • Take Love and Loyalty, mix it thoroughly with Faith.
  • Blend it with Tenderness, Kindness & Understanding.
  • Sprinkle abundantly with Laughter.
  • Bake it with Sunshine.
  • Serve daily with Generous helpings as toppings.
